Leonardo da Vinci

Leonardo da Vinci was an Italian polymath of the High Renaissance who is widely considered one of the most diversely talented individuals ever to have lived.

Some notable works:

  • Mona Lisa
  • The Last Supper
  • Vitruvian Man

Claude Monet

Claude Monet was a French painter and a founder of French Impressionist painting. His work gave a name to the movement Impressionism.

Some notable works:

  • Water Lilies series
  • Impression, Sunrise
  • Woman with a Parasol

Pablo Picasso

Pablo Picasso was a Spanish painter, sculptor, printmaker, and one of the most influential artists of the 20th century. He is known for co-founding the Cubist movement.

Some notable works:

  • Les Demoiselles d'Avignon
  • Guernica
  • The Weeping Woman
